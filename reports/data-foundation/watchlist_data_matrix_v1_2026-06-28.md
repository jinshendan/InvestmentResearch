## Scope and Source Rules

This is the first data-foundation matrix for the two-layer AI/technology watchlist. It is source-first and designed for later valuation / mispricing work. It is not an investment conclusion.

Inputs:
- Two-layer watchlist: `reports/watchlist/final_watchlist_two_layer_structure_2026-06-27.md`
- Evidence confirmation pass: `reports/evidence/watchlist_evidence_confirmation_task9_2026-06-27.md`
- Template / merge protocol: `reports/data-foundation/data_matrix_template_merge_protocol_v1_2026-06-28.md`

Labels:
- confirmed = official filing, company IR, company announcement, product docs, pricing docs, regulator/contract filing.
- reported = reputable media, credible data provider, third-party estimate.
- rumored = unverified / anonymous / secondary-market chatter.
- missing = needed for analysis but not yet reliably sourced.

Public-company price note:
- L1 listed target price / market cap / EV / multiples require a dedicated adjusted-close refresh before valuation. This v1 preserves evidence and gap structure; it does not fill stale or unverified market prices.
