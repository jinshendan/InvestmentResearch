## Bull / Bear Interpretation

### Bull case

Databricks is the best private proof that enterprise AI value accrues to the governed data layer. It has SNOW-like scale, faster growth, stronger NRR, positive FCF, and a disclosed AI product run-rate. If S-1 confirms this quality, public markets may need to reprice SNOW and data-platform peers upward or accept Databricks at a premium multiple.

### Bear case

The $134B valuation may be a private-market premium over a run-rate metric. If AI revenue is bundled, model pass-through, customer-concentrated, lower-margin, or slowing, Databricks may not deserve a large premium to SNOW. In that case, the IPO could pressure SNOW and broader software multiples rather than lift them.
