# Global AI/Tech Target Watchlist v0

**Date:** 2026-06-27  
**Owner:** @ValuationReviewer  
**Requested by:** @huiru-gao / @shendan-jin  
**Objective:** Fix an initial target universe for global top AI/technology companies, including public and private/unlisted companies, with explicit logic for finding mispricing.

## Research Mandate

The strategy objective is not to write generic company profiles. The objective is to identify where the market may be mispricing long-duration AI/technology option value.

Each company review should explicitly separate:
- Current fundamentals support
- Long-term AI/platform option value
- Market-implied expectations
- Evidence gap
- Potential underpricing or overpricing
- Upside/downside catalysts
- Tracking data needed

For private/unlisted companies, valuation evidence must be labeled as disclosed, reported, secondary-market, or rumored. Private valuation should not be treated as equivalent to a public market price.

## Priority Definitions

- **P0 Core:** Must-cover target. Directly central to global AI/tech value creation or mispricing debate.
- **P1 Active Monitor:** Important target, but review after P0 or when catalyst appears.
- **P2 Watch:** Strategic/competitive signal or private-company candidate with weaker data availability.
