## Mandate

The research target universe is global top AI and technology companies, including public companies and private/unlisted companies with potential listing, investment opportunity, or major industry impact.

The strategy goal is to find mispricing, not to write generic company profiles. Each company review must separate:
- Current fundamentals support
- Market-implied expectations
- Long-term AI/platform option value
- Evidence quality and gaps
- Upside/downside catalysts
- Tracking data

## Canonical Scope Decisions

- China private/unlisted AI companies stay in the watchlist.
- SpaceX/Starlink stays in the main AI/tech watchlist.
- P1 public companies are included in first-batch coverage.
- Public-company price history should use adjusted close / 前复权.
- Private-company valuations must be labeled as company-disclosed, reported, secondary-market, or rumored.
- Private-company marks should not be treated as equivalent to public market capitalization without liquidity, disclosure, and exit-path caveats.
